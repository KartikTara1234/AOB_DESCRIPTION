about this item:

The AOB Assistant on Blink is a groundbreaking application designed to bridge the communication gap between patients who are unable to express themselves verbally and their caregivers or medical professionals. It addresses the crucial need to understand and cater to the needs of differently-abled individuals who face challenges in vocalizing their discomfort or requirements due to various medical conditions.

At its core, the AOB Assistant on Blink operates by detecting complex movements i.e retina , enabling patients to navigate through a list of pre-defined options using an LCD interface connected to a MacBook. This intuitive interface allows patients to communicate their needs or discomfort effectively without the need for verbal communication.
